Pasta all’Arrabbiata: A Spicy Italian Classic

Pasta all’Arrabbiata is a classic Italian pasta dish from Rome. The name means “angry pasta” because of the spicy heat from dried red chili peppers. This simple dish is known for its bold flavors and quick preparation.

The beauty of Pasta all’Arrabbiata is its simplicity and perfect balance of spice and tomato.

- Italian Cuisine Expert

Ingredients:

  • Penne Pasta: 400g
  • Tomatoes: 1 can
  • Garlic: 3 cloves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Dried Red Chili Peppers: To taste

Preparation:

  1. Boil salted water and cook the pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic and chili peppers in olive oil.
  3. Add tomatoes and let the sauce simmer.
  4. Toss the pasta with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4 teaspoon of chili flakes adds extra heat.
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil enhances flavor.
